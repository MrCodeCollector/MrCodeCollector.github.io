ABOUT

I'm a self taught programmer, seeking to generate change in the world around me. Towards the end of High School, I realized that I was passionate about Software Engineering and Education. It was then that I started the first Computer Science club at my school. Upon graduating, I was offered a position to teach AP Computer Science through TEALS, a Microsoft Philanthropy. Helping other people through what I create has always been something I have cared about. I want to change the way people think about education, and bring ideas that people can benefit from, to life. Currently, I am working on a product with a fellow student at the Make School: Product University, that alters the way we give and receive feedback in the classroom. By creating a semi-autonomous feedback platform on the web and iOS, my partner and I plan to push instructors and students alike to reach their maximum potential.
